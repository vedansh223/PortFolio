Summary of the Project:-

Our project focuses on utilizing the capabilities of IBM Cognos, a powerful business intelligence and analytics platform, to perform data analytics on COVID-19 data. The objective is to gain insights and generate meaningful visualizations that can aid decision-making and understanding of the pandemic's impact.The project begins by integrating relevant COVID-19 datasets from various sources, such as public health databases, research institutions, and internal data. This data includes information on cases, deaths, hospitalizations, testing rates, vaccination rates, and other key metrics. IBM Cognos allows us to connect and consolidate these datasets efficiently.
